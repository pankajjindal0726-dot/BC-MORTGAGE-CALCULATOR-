üìã BC Mortgage Rules (2025)

  • Down Payment: 5% on first $500k, 10% on $500k-$1.5M, 20% on $1.5M+
  • CMHC Insurance: Required for down payments under 20% (0.6% to 4.5% premium)
  • 30-Year Amortization: Available for first-time buyers and new builds
  • Property Transfer Tax: 1% on first $200k, 2% on $200k-$2M, 3% on $2M-$3M, 5% above $3M
  • First-Time Buyer PTT Exemption: Full exemption up to $835,000, partial up to $860,000
  • Stress Test: You must qualify at your rate + 2% or the Bank of Canada rate
